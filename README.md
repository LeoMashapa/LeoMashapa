# About Me

There is a moment every analyst experiences.

You build the report. The data is accurate. The dashboard looks professional. Every chart tells part of the story.

Then someone asks:

**"What should we do with this information?"**

That question is why I chose data analytics.

My name is **Leo Mashapa**, a Data Analyst passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights that help businesses make smarter decisions, improve performance, and uncover new opportunities.

- Over 3 years of experience in financial services, banking, and technology environments.

- Experienced in reporting, performance monitoring, and analyzing business data to support operational and strategic decision-making.

- Skilled in Power BI, SQL, Python, Excel, and data visualization, turning complex datasets into clear, actionable insights.

- Currently building expertise in Business Intelligence and analytics consulting to help organizations become more data-driven.

My background has taught me that data alone does not create value. Value comes from understanding the story behind the numbers and translating that story into actions that drive results.

Where I work best:

- Organizations looking to move from reactive reporting to proactive decision-making.

- Teams that need clear, business-focused insights rather than technical jargon.

- Projects that require transforming large volumes of data into meaningful business intelligence.

- Leaders who want visibility into performance, trends, risks, and opportunities.

I believe great analytics is not about creating more reports—it's about helping people make better decisions with confidence.

**Turning data into direction. Turning insights into impact.**
